Delete Your Account & Data

We respect your right to delete your data. You can remove your My Digital Diary account and all associated data using any of the methods below.

Method 1 — Delete from within the app

1 Open My Digital Diary on your phone

2 Go to More tab (bottom right)

3 Scroll down to Cloud Sync section

4 Tap "Delete my account & data"

5 Confirm deletion when prompted

This immediately deletes:

  • All your cloud data (journal entries, expenses, time logs, budgets)
  • All local data on the device
  • Your sign-in session

Method 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

📧 Request Deletion by Email

Please include the Google account email address you used to sign in. We will delete all your data from our servers within 30 days and confirm by email.

Method 3 — Uninstall the app

Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device. To also delete cloud data, please use Method 1 or 2 above.

What gets deleted:
• All journal entries and diary sections
• All expense and income records
• All time tracking entries and projects
• Budget settings and custom categories
• Backup files stored on device
• Your sign-in connection to My Digital Diary

What is NOT affected:
• Your Google account itself (we do not manage your Google account)
• Data you exported to Word, Excel, or PDF files (those are saved on your device)
